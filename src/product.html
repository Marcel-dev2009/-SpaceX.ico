STARSHIP


The SpaceX Starship is a fully reusable spacecraft designed for transporting humans and cargo to destinations
like the Moon, Mars, and beyond. It features a two-stage system: the Starship upper stage and the
Super Heavy booster. Starship aims to enable interplanetary travel and is central to SpaceX's long-term vision of colonizing Mars.
Its first orbital test flight occurred in April 2023, showcasing its potential for future space missions.

SPACESUITS

CATEGORIES

CATEGORIES


IVM and EVM Activity Space Suits


IVM and EVM Activity Space Suits


IVM { IntraVehicular Mobility }


IVM { IntraVehicular Mobility }


IVM suits are designed for activities inside spacecraft or space stations. Their primary uses include:

Thermal protection: Maintaining a stable temperature in environments with variable conditions.
Mobility: Allowing astronauts to move freely while performing tasks.
Life support: Providing oxygen and removing carbon dioxide during operations inside the vehicle.


EVM { ExtraVehicular Mobility }


EVM { ExtraVehicular Mobility }


EVM suits, also known as EVA (Extravehicular Activity) suits, are meant for activities outside the spacecraft, such as spacewalks. Key uses include:

Environmental protection: Protecting astronauts from the harsh conditions of space, including extreme temperatures and micrometeoroids.
Life support: Offering a self-contained life support system for breathing and temperature control during spacewalks.
Mobility and tools: Enabling movement and the use of tools in a zero-gravity environment.


Both types of suits are crucial for ensuring astronaut safety and facilitating various operations in the unique environments of space.

HOW IT WORKS


Starlink works by deploying thousands of small satellites
